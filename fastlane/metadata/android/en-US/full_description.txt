T9 Launcher is a fast, keyboard-driven app launcher for Android that uses T9-style input to search and launch your apps in seconds.

Triggered by a home-screen shortcut, the launcher appears as a translucent overlay card anchored near your tap position. Instead of scrolling app drawers or typing on a full keyboard, you search using the numeric keypad — just like T9 predictive text. Each number key (2–9) maps to a set of letters, and the launcher filters your installed apps in real time as you press digits.

The match is a substring search over the app name that ignores spaces and other delimiters (-, _, .), so a digit sequence can span across them. Examples:
- 9 4 2 matches "WhatsApp"
- 9 7 matches "Play Store" (across the space)
- 3 3 7 matches "F-Droid" (across the dash)

Matched characters are highlighted (yellow background, bold). Apps are sorted by launch frequency over the last 10 days, then alphabetically, so your most-used apps surface first.

Features:
- 3×3 numeric keypad (2–9) plus Clear
- Long-press Clear to open Settings
- Long-press an app to access App Info or Uninstall
- Optional: highlight recently launched / recently installed apps
- Optional: extend search to app descriptions

Privacy:
- No internet permission, no analytics, no telemetry
- All launch history and preferences are stored locally in SharedPreferences
- No data leaves your device

Requires Android 13 (API 33) or higher.
Available in English and Italian.
